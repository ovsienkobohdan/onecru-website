about

onecru is a street designers team based in Kyiv. Was founded in early 2014 by couple artists Smek and Shan. Our field of activity is not limited to walls in the streets - onecru touches and combines elements of graffiti, design and illustration in a living and digital format. Such a mixture makes our style unique and all-embracing. Our creativity bears the subtlety of forms, colors and lines, aesthetics of the current time and place. We want not only to create stylish and beautiful things, but also to cultivate a taste culture in Ukraine and the world.
